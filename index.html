Hi!

Timothy John Berners-Lee was born on June 8, 1955, in LEngland. He is one of four children of Mary Lee Woods and Conway Berners-Lee. By 1989, he began working on his information sharing system through an application protocol called HTTP.
This Hypertext Transfer Protocol became the venue to transfer and share information over the Internet using an address known as URL or Uniform Resource Locator.
In 1990, he married Nancy Carlson, with whom he had two children. The couple divorced in 2001 and Berners-Lee married Rosemary Leith in 2014.
